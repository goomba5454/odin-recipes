Pork Chops!

Homepage

Description

Delightfully breaded and baked and baked in the oven until crispy and juicy.

  • Pork Chop cutlets
  • Mayonaise
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Pat dry the pork cutlets and rub with mayonaise
  2. Dip the pork cutlets in bread crumbs. Try to apply bread crumbs evenly to all sides
  3. Place on a greased, foil lined sheet and bake in the oven at 400F for 25 minutes
