Carbonara!

Homepage

Description

A very easy to make recipe to yield serviceable results!

  • Pasta of choice
  • Bacon
  • Garlic
  • Eggs
  • Parmasean cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta as directed on box
  2. Cook bacon in pan. When bacon is almost done, throw in some garlic and cook for one minute more. Set aside and drain fat from pan
  3. Whisk eggs and cheese together in bowl, then pour over hot drained pasta
